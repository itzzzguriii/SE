1. Full concentration while doing the group project 
As we are in a group project, we should give full concentration while doing the project
so that we know every part of our project, how it works and when to use specific 
features etc.
2.Trying best to co-operate 
Try your best to co-operate. 
3. Do not make excuses
Excuses will not be tolerated. Try to finish your task within the time allocated for you. If 
you find difficulties, feel free to ask your teammates. Ask for help and support.
4. Respect each other's opinion and understand different perspectives
As human beings, we all have different perspectives and opinions. We may not always 
agree on different decisions, but we can always share our point of view. Please be 
respectful while expressing your thoughts.
5. Encourage everyone 
As this is our first project, we may face many difficulties while completing our work. 
During this time, we should always encourage our teammates and give additional 
support when needed.
